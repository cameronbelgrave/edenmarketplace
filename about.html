CEO/Founder

Eden Marketplace was founded by Cameron Belgrave as a final project for his Environmental Science : Sustainability course at The Masters School, taught by Eric Shear.

Shipping

Eden Marketplace participates in a carbon neutral shipping program. By offseting the emmisions associated with shipping by planting trees, we ensure that while we are contionuing to produce CO2 emmisions through our shipments, we are consciously making the decision to offset those emisions. We also solely use packaging made from recyclable, high recycled content materials, and earth friendly tapes.

Product Life Cycle

As creators ourselves, we understand that each of our products has its own unique life cycle, and we make sure to provide as much information we have on that cycle as possible. From the designing process, to the final staging process, being able to retrace the life cycle of every product is a testament to the fact that as distributors of sustainable products, we have nothing to hide.
